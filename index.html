Southwest Airlines

WELCOME TO OUR NEW BRAND BOOK.

For nearly 60 years, Southwest Airlines has been a trailblazer in the airline industry, known for our love, freedom, and no-frills approach to air travel. We've built a strong foundation rooted in transparency and customer-first service, but as the industry evolves, so must we. While we've cultivated a loyal following, challenges like maintaining our brand equity and modernizing perceptions require a renewed focus and unified effort. This brand book is designed to help us meet that moment.

This resource acts as a supplement to our official brand guidelines, which can be found here. Together, these tools provide the framework we need to confidently represent Southwest's unique identity across all channels.

The purpose of this brand book is to aid in Southwest's transition to its next chapter: becoming bigger, bolder, and even more innovative. This interactive, tech-forward resource offers a streamlined introduction to everything the Southwest brand stands for. Employees can turn to it for a quick, inspirational reminder of our values, voice, and vision—essential as we continue to modernize and lead with clarity and confidence.

As the airline industry grows more competitive, this brand book empowers us to embrace innovation while staying grounded in the principles that define us. It is designed to foster alignment across teams, ensure consistency across communications, and inspire creativity. Through the adoption of this guide, we aim to position Southwest not just as an industry player, but as a leader in cutting-edge excellence and customer-first innovation.

Southwest Airlines has always been more than an airline—it's a symbol of connection, optimism, and fun. This brand book is our tool for staying true to that legacy while embracing the opportunities that lie ahead. Together, we'll continue to build on our rich history, ensuring the Southwest brand remains as bold and dynamic as the people and journeys we serve.

Welcome to the next chapter of Southwest.
