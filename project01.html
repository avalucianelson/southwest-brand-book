Our History

In nearly 60 years of service, we at Southwest Airlines have built our brand around love, freedom, and a no-frills, fun-loving approach to air travel. From our beginnings as a small Texas airline, we’ve grown into one of the most recognized and beloved names in the industry. Our messaging has consistently emphasized our values, from heart-filled imagery to humorous, relatable advertising campaigns. Whether through our iconic “LUV” branding or the stories we share from our customers and crew, we’ve remained committed to creating meaningful, personal connections. This dedication to transparency, affordability, and exceptional service continues to guide us as we make travel enjoyable for everyone.
