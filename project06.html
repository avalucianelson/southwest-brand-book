Our Imagery

At Southwest Airlines, our imagery reflects the heart and humanity of our brand. We prioritize visuals that feel authentic, modern, and approachable, capturing moments that resonate with our customers and team members. Our photography is candid and honest, showcasing real people, real connections, and real experiences—whether it’s a heartfelt interaction between crew and passengers or the joy of travel itself. We aim for a balance of spontaneity and polish, ensuring that our images remain inviting, relatable, and beautiful. Through every photograph, we strive to tell stories that highlight our fun-LUVing spirit, dedication to people, and commitment to creating meaningful journeys.

Key Imagery Guidelines

ANY PHOTOS OR IMAGERY WE USE SHOULD BE 1) OPTIMISTIC, 2) CLEAN AND MINIMAL, 3) NATURAL, AND 4) COMMUNICATE THE SOUTHWEST SPIRIT.

People in Imagery

WE PUT PEOPLE FIRST. AS IS SUCH, THE KEY OBJECTIVE OF OUR COMMUNICATION IS TO CONNECT WITH THE CUSTOMER. THEREFORE, IT'S IMPORTANT THAT OUR CAPTURED MOMENTS ARE RELATABLE TO THEM. PHOTOGRAPHS SHOULD CAPTURE GENUINE EXPERIENCES, SINCERE EMOTION, AND ORGANIC EXPRESSIONS. EVERYTHING SHOULD FEEL REAL, NOT OVERLY STAGED.

OUR IMAGERY CAPTURES THE HEART, HUMANITY, AND JOY OF SOUTHWEST, TELLING STORIES THAT BRING OUR BRAND TO LIFE AND REFLECT THE CONNECTIONS WE CREATE EVERY DAY.
